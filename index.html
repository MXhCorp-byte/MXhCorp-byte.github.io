Welcome!

Welcome to PriceCheck PK — a student-led initiative created to promote fair pricing and transparency in Pakistan. Many shopkeepers change prices based on the customer, which leads to overcharging and confusion. To help solve this, we have started collecting and listing the actual market prices of everyday essential products like meat, poultry, vegetables, and fruits. Our goal is to give people a clear idea of fair prices so they can shop with confidence and avoid being exploited. This platform is just the beginning, and we hope to expand it with more categories over time.
